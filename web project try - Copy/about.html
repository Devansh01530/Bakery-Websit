Our Story

Passion, tradition, and a whole lot of butter 🧈 — the story of how Fresh Bakes came to life.

🏪

A Bakery Born from Passion

Fresh Bakes was founded in 2015 by Nyati — a passionate home baker who turned a love for baking into a full-fledged neighbourhood bakery. What began as weekend experiments in a small kitchen in Ghaziabad quickly became the talk of the town.

Nyati's philosophy has always been simple — use only the freshest natural ingredients, bake everything from scratch every single day, and put love into every single product that leaves the kitchen. No shortcuts. No compromises.

Today, Fresh Bakes serves hundreds of happy customers every week — from custom birthday cakes to freshly baked sourdough — and has grown into a trusted name for celebrations and everyday indulgences alike.
